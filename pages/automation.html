Concept 1

What is automation?

When software does a repetitive task for you — automatically, every time, without you touching it.

The golden rule

Every automation = 3 parts

No matter how complex a workflow looks, it always comes down to the same three building blocks.

⚡
01 — Trigger
What starts it?
An event that n8n listens for. When it happens — the entire workflow wakes up and begins running.
"A new email arrived with a PDF attachment"
🔀
02 — Logic
What decides?
Optional conditions and decisions your workflow makes. Filters, IF/ELSE checks, routing data down different paths.
"If the sender is from a university domain…"
✅
03 — Action
What happens?
The task that actually gets done. The thing that used to take your time — now handled automatically in milliseconds.
"Save PDF + log in Sheets + send reply"
A real automation flow — form submission to confirmation email
